Who We Are

We are a team of passionate AIML engineering students from BMS College of Engineering, Bengaluru, driven to harness the power of technology to make a positive impact on healthcare accessibility and awareness.

As students specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, we bring a unique blend of technical expertise and a desire to solve real-world challenges. Our goal is to create impactful solutions that improve health literacy and accessibility.

Our Vision

Our vision is to bridge the gap between healthcare and technology, making medical information and resources accessible to everyone, empowering individuals to make informed health decisions.

We believe that everyone deserves access to reliable health information and guidance, regardless of their location or background. Through our AI-powered platform, we aim to democratize healthcare knowledge and support.

Our Mission

Our mission is to develop a platform powered by AI and ML models capable of assisting users in identifying potential health concerns based on reported symptoms.

We aim to simplify health education by offering reliable resources for better understanding and decision-making. Our platform leverages machine learning models trained on comprehensive health datasets to provide accurate predictions based on user input.

Your Well-being, Our Priority

We prioritize the well-being of every individual using our platform.

Understanding healthcare can be complex, so we strive to offer not only predictions but also informative content, including precautions, medications, dietary suggestions, and wellness tips. We invite you to explore our platform, engage with our educational resources, and join us in transforming how healthcare information is accessed and understood.
